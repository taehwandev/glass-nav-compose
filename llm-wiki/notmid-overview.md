# notmid Overview

## Product

`notmid` is an open-source Android/web product for discovering places through short videos, map context, and place-aware chat.

Tagline:

```text
not mid. show receipts.
```

The app should feel like a real Gen Z local discovery service, not a component sample or marketing landing page.

## Product Loop

1. Watch a short clip tied to a real place.
2. Check freshness, distance, vibe, and proof.
3. Save it, open it on the map, or share it into chat.
4. Visit and post a receipt clip.

## Top-Level Screens

## UX Direction

- First viewport is the app shell, not a landing page.
- Video and map are primary canvases.
- Glass is functional: navigation, cards, controls, composers, sheets.
- Browsing can work signed out.
- Capture, save, chat, and profile edit require auth.
- Firebase and open-source affordances belong in settings/onboarding, not the primary consumer flow.

## Naming And Voice

- Product name is lowercase `notmid`.
- Voice can be internet-native: `show receipts`, `pull up?`, `live rn`, `worth it`.
- Keep UI practical. Do not let tone become childish or purely decorative.
